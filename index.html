About Mental Health

Mental health affects how we think, feel, and act. Understanding it helps us build healthier, more balanced lives.

Common Issues

Recognising early signs can help you or someone you care about get support sooner.

Emotional Changes

Feeling overwhelmed, unusually sad, anxious, or irritable for long periods.

Behavioural Changes

Withdrawing from friends, losing interest in activities, or changes in sleep.

Physical Changes

Low energy, headaches, appetite changes, or unexplained aches and pains.

Tips for Wellbeing

Small daily habits can make a big difference in your mental wellbeing.

  • Practice deep breathing or meditation
  • Stay connected with friends and family
  • Get regular sleep and exercise
  • Take breaks when you feel overwhelmed
  • Talk to someone you trust about how you feel
